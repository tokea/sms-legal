How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number solely to send automated trading position alerts, risk notifications, and operational updates related to your account activity.

SMS Alerts & Consent

By providing your mobile phone number in the application settings and selecting the opt-in checkbox, you consent to receive automated SMS alerts from this service. These alerts include trading position updates, risk threshold breaches, and profit target notifications.

  • Opt-in: Users provide their phone number and explicitly enable SMS alerts within the application settings.
  • Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on market conditions and active trading positions. You may receive multiple messages per day during market hours.
  • Opt-out: You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Message and data rates may apply.

SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell user data
  • Share user data with third parties for marketing
  • Send promotional or marketing SMS messages
