TAMD LLC provides automated trading alert services. Our SMS alert system delivers real-time position monitoring updates, risk threshold notifications, and profit target alerts to account holders.

SMS Alert Service

Account holders opt in to receive SMS alerts by providing their mobile phone number in the application settings and enabling SMS notifications. Messages are sent only to the account holder. Message frequency varies based on market conditions and active trading positions.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP. Message and data rates may apply.
