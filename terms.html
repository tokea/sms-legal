By providing your mobile phone number in the application settings and selecting the opt-in checkbox to enable SMS alerts, you agree to receive automated system notification messages related to your trading account, including position alerts, risk threshold breaches, and profit target notifications.

Message frequency varies based on market conditions and active trading positions. You may receive multiple messages per day during market hours (typically Monday–Friday, 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET).

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive no further SMS messages unless you re-enable alerts in the application settings.
